How To Buy

1

Set Up Your Wallet

Setup your wallet using metamask or trustwallet to get started. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.
2

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $REYA. If you don’t have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.



3

Buy & Swap For $REYA    

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $REYA token address into Uniswap, select Reborn Yahwae, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.





4

Buy Enjoy The Ride

After the above step there is no need for more work, JUST HODL and ENJOY the ride to the moon.
